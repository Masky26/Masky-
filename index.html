I am a Petroleum Geoscientist with hands-on experience across exploration and production workflows in Nigeria's upstream sector. My work spans 3D seismic interpretation, well log analysis, petrophysical evaluation, and static reservoir modelling — with a strong emphasis on translating subsurface data into actionable decisions.

At Chevron Nigeria Limited, I conducted detailed subsurface analysis across multiple fault blocks, generating pay zone cross-sections, fluid distribution maps, and shale distribution plots to support field development planning. Prior to that, at Reigh-Shore Energy Services, I delivered end-to-end G&G software support, seismic data management, and reservoir evaluation services for NAPIMS-FES clients.

I combine geoscience expertise with data analytics capabilities, enabling me to bring both technical rigour and quantitative insight to subsurface challenges. I am actively seeking new opportunities where I can contribute to high-impact exploration and development projects.
